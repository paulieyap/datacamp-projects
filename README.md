# 🎓 DataCamp Projects

A collection of hands-on ML and data science projects completed as part of the
**THRIVE ML Specialization** by Kiron Open Higher Education (2026).

These projects complement my professional experience at Procter & Gamble and
DHL Consulting by reinforcing the theoretical and applied foundations of the
full ML lifecycle, from classical machine learning through to deep learning and LLMs.
